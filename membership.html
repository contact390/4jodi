Frequently Asked Questions

How does Hitiashi verify profiles?

We use a 4-step verification process including document verification, background checks, personal interviews, and social media validation to ensure all profiles are genuine.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade anytime. The remaining value of your current plan will be applied to your upgrade.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never share your contact details without permission.

Can I hide my profile from others?

Yes, you can activate incognito mode to hide your profile until you're ready to go public.

Do you offer refunds?

We provide refunds only in specific cases based on our terms and conditions. Please contact support for details.

How do I delete my profile?

Go to account settings and select "Delete Profile." Confirm your decision and your data will be permanently removed.
