Frequently Asked Questions (NRI)

1. How can NRIs register on Hitaishi Matrimony?
NRIs can register online using their email and phone number. Just select your country code and fill in your profile to get started.
2. Is the platform secure for international users?
Yes, Hitaishi Matrimony uses advanced encryption and manual verification to ensure all profiles are genuine and secure.
3. Can I search based on community or language preferences?
Absolutely! You can filter profiles based on community, language, location, profession, and more.
4. What if I need help during registration or search?
You can reach our dedicated NRI support team by email or phone for personalized guidance throughout your journey.
5. Is there any fee for using the service?
Basic registration is free. We also offer premium plans with additional features like contact view, express interest, and more.
6. Can I access Hitaishi Matrimony from any country?
Yes! Our platform is globally accessible. You can connect and communicate with users from India or other countries anytime.
